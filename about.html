Our Story

BINA began in 2023 when founder Bimpe Adegoke, a veteran educator, noticed how students struggled with common entrance exam preparation. Many students had limited access to quality practice materials, especially in remote areas.

While teaching at a primary school in Lagos, Bimpe observed that students who practiced regularly with past questions performed significantly better. However, printed materials were expensive and quickly became outdated.

The solution came through collaboration with tech specialist Ike Nnamdi. Together, they created a digital platform that would make exam practice accessible to all Nigerian students through the devices their families already owned.

Today, BINA is growing into a comprehensive educational tool that helps thousands of Nigerian children prepare effectively for one of the most important examinations in their academic journey.
